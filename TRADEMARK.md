# NextSpark Trademark Policy

## Trademark Notice

"NextSpark", the NextSpark logo, and related marks are trademarks of NextSpark. This policy explains how you may use these trademarks.

## What you CAN do

- ✅ Use NextSpark to build your own applications and SaaS products
- ✅ Say "Built with NextSpark" or "Powered by NextSpark" in your projects
- ✅ Reference NextSpark in articles, tutorials, and documentation
- ✅ Use the name when discussing or reviewing the project
- ✅ Fork the project for your own use

## What you CANNOT do

- ❌ Use "NextSpark" in your product name (e.g., "NextSpark Pro", "SuperNextSpark")
- ❌ Use the NextSpark logo as your product's logo
- ❌ Create a fork and call it "NextSpark" or any confusingly similar name
- ❌ Imply official endorsement or affiliation without permission
- ❌ Use the trademarks in domain names (e.g., nextspark-themes.com)
- ❌ Sell products or services using the NextSpark name/logo

## Naming your fork

If you fork NextSpark, you must:
1. Choose a different name that doesn't include "NextSpark"
2. Use your own logo and branding
3. Clearly state it's a fork, not the original project

## Examples

| Allowed | Not Allowed |
|---------|-------------|
| "My SaaS (built with NextSpark)" | "NextSpark Enterprise Edition" |
| "A NextSpark tutorial" | "Official NextSpark Themes" |
| Blog post reviewing NextSpark | nextspark-plugins.com |

When using "Built with NextSpark", we appreciate (but don't require) a link to https://nextspark.dev
